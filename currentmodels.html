Current Models

Aventador

Launched in 2012, the Aventador is the latest in Lamborghini's mid-engined V12's. With variants reaching nearly 800 horsepower, it is the most powerful and aggressive bull in the Lamborghini stable.

Huracan

The Huracan is Lamborghini's V10 variant. Less powerful but far more nimble than its big brother the Aventador, the Huracan is the perfect choice for burning rubber and setting records on the track.

Urus

Launched in 2018, the Urus is a venture into new spaces for Lamborghini. It is a V8 SUV that is already a major success in North American markets, revitalizing every facet of the morning routine for suburban soccer moms!
